Why It Matters

The Problem

Road defects like potholes and unmarked speed bumps increase accident risks and cost drivers thousands in repairs annually.

Our Impact

RASD gives drivers real-time alerts while helping governments plan smarter maintenance and create safer roads for everyone.

33% Accident Reduction
Lower Maintenance Costs

RASD Features

Real-Time Detection

LiDAR + Camera fusion instantly identifies potholes & speed bumps.

Driver Alerts

LED guidance: Green = Safe, Orange = Slow down, Red = Danger.

Cloud Dashboard

GPS-tagged hazards uploaded to a citywide dashboard for authorities.

Project Development Steps

1

Preparation

Setting up the environment, collecting datasets, and preparing training data for AI model development.

2

Sprint 1: Detecting

Training computer vision models to detect potholes and speed bumps using LiDAR and camera data fusion.

3

Sprint 2: Alerting

Integrating sensors, cameras, and GPS modules with real-time LED alerts to notify drivers of road hazards.

4

Sprint 3: Dashboard

Developing a cloud-based dashboard for authorities to monitor GPS-tagged hazards and plan maintenance actions.

5

Final Presentation

Conducting real-world testing, refining the system, and presenting the solution for deployment.
